
# PVA+ Quick Start Guide

Welcome to PVA+, your AI Personal Assistant that works seamlessly across all your devices!

## 🚀 Getting Started

### 1. Installation

**iOS (iPhone/iPad)**
- Open App Store
- Search for "PVA+"
- Tap "Get" to download
- Open the app

**Android (Phone/Tablet)**
- Open Google Play Store
- Search for "PVA+"
- Tap "Install"
- Open the app

**Web/Desktop**
- Visit https://app.pvaplus.com
- Sign in with your account
- Add to home screen for quick access

### 2. Create Your Account

Choose your preferred sign-in method:

- **Google**: Quick sign-in with your Google account
- **Apple**: Sign in with Apple ID (iOS only)
- **Microsoft**: Use your Microsoft account
- **Email**: Create account with email and password

### 3. Grant Permissions

For the best experience, allow:

- ✅ **Notifications**: Get reminders and updates
- ✅ **Microphone**: Use voice commands
- ✅ **Calendar**: Schedule meetings automatically
- ✅ **Contacts**: Quick access to people

You can change these anytime in Settings.

## ⚙️ Settings

### Customize Your Experience

**Profile → Settings**

- **Theme**: Light, Dark, or Auto
- **Language**: 20+ languages supported
- **Voice**: Choose voice and accent
- **Notifications**: Customize alerts
- **Privacy**: Control data sharing
- **Integrations**: Connect apps

### AI Configuration

**Profile → AI Configuration**

- **Autonomy Level**: Suggest-Only, Semi-Autonomous, or Fully-Autonomous
- **AI Services**: Configure OpenAI, LangChain, Pinecone
- **Voice Services**: Set up Whisper, ElevenLabs
- **Travel APIs**: Connect Amadeus, Skyscanner

### Privacy & Security

**Profile → Privacy & Security**

- **Data Collection**: Control what's tracked
- **Analytics**: Opt in/out of usage data
- **Location**: Enable/disable location tracking
- **Voice Recording**: Manage voice data
- **Audit Log**: See all data access

## 🆘 Troubleshooting

### App Not Syncing

1. Check internet connection
2. Tap sync button in top right
3. Go to Profile → Sync Now
4. Restart app if needed

### Notifications Not Working

1. Check notification permissions
2. Disable quiet hours temporarily
3. Test notification in settings
4. Reinstall app if needed

### Voice Not Working

1. Check microphone permission
2. Test microphone in settings
3. Try different wake word
4. Restart app

### Login Issues

1. Verify email/password
2. Try "Forgot Password"
3. Use different sign-in method
4. Contact support
