
# Digital Body Language - Quick Start Guide

## What is Digital Body Language?

The **Digital Body Language (DBL) Engine** is your AI assistant's "sixth sense" - it reads your digital behavioral signals to understand your current state and proactively help you.

Think of it as having a personal assistant who knows when you're:
- 🧠 Deep in focus and shouldn't be disturbed
- 😰 Overwhelmed and need help managing tasks
- ✈️ Planning a trip and could use booking assistance
- 📞 In a meeting and need auto-responses enabled

---

## Quick Setup (5 minutes)

### Step 1: Enable Input Streams

Navigate to **Digital Body Language** screen and grant permissions for:

- ✅ **Calendar Events** - Understand your meeting schedule
- ✅ **Email Patterns** - Track response times (not content!)
- ✅ **Device Activity** - Monitor unlock patterns
- ✅ **App Usage** - Detect focus vs. distraction

**Privacy Note**: All data is processed locally and encrypted. You can revoke permissions anytime.

### Step 2: Choose Autonomy Level

Select how much control you want to give the assistant:

- **Suggest-Only**: Assistant suggests actions, you approve each one
- **Semi-Autonomous**: Assistant handles low-impact actions automatically
- **Fully-Autonomous**: Maximum automation (recommended after 1-2 weeks)

**Recommendation**: Start with "Suggest-Only" for the first week.

### Step 3: Let It Learn

Use your device normally for 3-5 days. The engine needs to learn your patterns:
- Your typical working hours
- Your meeting schedule
- Your response patterns
- Your focus times

## Understanding Your States

The engine recognizes 9 states:

| State | What It Means | Interruptibility |
|-------|---------------|------------------|
| 🌙 **Idle** | Not actively working | High |
| 🧠 **Focus-Deep** | Deep concentration | Low |
| 🎯 **Focus-Shallow** | Working but distractible | Medium |
| ✈️ **Travelling** | In transit | Medium |
| 📞 **InCall** | On a call | Low |
| 👥 **InMeeting** | In a meeting | Low |
| 😰 **Overloaded** | Stressed and overwhelmed | Low |
| 🗓️ **ReadyForBooking** | Planning a trip | High |
| ✅ **Available** | Ready to engage | High |

---

## Example Actions by State

### When Overloaded 😰
- Postpone non-urgent notifications
- Summarize meeting updates
- Suggest taking a break
- Auto-reply to low-priority emails

### When in Focus-Deep 🧠
- Enable Do Not Disturb
- Block calendar for focus time
- Postpone all notifications
- Silence non-critical alerts

### When in Meeting 👥
- Prepare meeting briefing
- Send status updates
- Auto-reply to messages
- Summarize action items

### When Ready for Booking 🗓️
- Search travel options
- Compare prices
- Suggest booking times
- Auto-book (with approval)

## Providing Feedback

Help the engine learn faster by providing feedback:

### After Each Action
- 👍 **Approve**: Action was helpful
- 👎 **Reject**: Action was not helpful
- ✏️ **Modify**: Action was close but needs adjustment

### Why Feedback Matters
- Improves accuracy from 70% → 95%+ in 2 weeks
- Personalizes to your unique workflow
- Reduces false positives
- Builds trust in automation

## Privacy & Security

### What We Track
- ✅ Calendar event times (not titles or details)
- ✅ Email response latency (not content)
- ✅ Device unlock patterns
- ✅ App categories (not specific apps)

### What We DON'T Track
- ❌ Email content
- ❌ Message content
- ❌ Browsing history
- ❌ Personal files
- ❌ Passwords or credentials

### Your Rights
- 📊 **Inspect**: View all collected data anytime
- 🗑️ **Delete**: One-tap data deletion
- 📤 **Export**: Download your data in JSON
- 🔒 **Control**: Granular permission controls

## Performance Metrics

Track your engine's performance:

- **Accuracy**: % of correct state predictions
- **Precision**: Quality of predictions
- **Recall**: Coverage of actual states
- **F1 Score**: Overall performance

**Target**: 90%+ accuracy after 2 weeks of use

## FAQ

**Q: Is my data shared with third parties?**
A: No. All processing is local. No data is sold or shared.

**Q: Can I use this without calendar access?**
A: Yes, but accuracy will be lower. Calendar is the most important signal.

**Q: How long until it's accurate?**
A: 3-5 days for basic patterns, 2 weeks for high accuracy.

**Q: Can I disable specific actions?**
A: Yes, all actions can be individually disabled in settings.

**Q: Does this work offline?**
A: Yes, all inference is done locally on-device.

**Q: What about battery life?**
A: Minimal impact (<2% per day) with optimized processing.
